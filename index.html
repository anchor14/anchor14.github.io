I set up this site for the bookchain project

These days, everyone is talking about blockchain. Unfortunately, I don't know much about it. But I am interested in books. So Nikolaj Ramsdal Nielsen, a good friend of mine and I decided that we can do something similar.

The Bookchain

The bookchain is quite a simple concept. One person reads a book, signs it on the title page. Then the person who just read it signs the book and gives it to the next person. Once the book reaches 20 signatures, it is planned to go back to the first reader. Just imagine what it would be like to realize that the book you loved traveled to distant places held in the hands of so many people and was loved. Isn't that a bit more exciting than a bitcoin that became three times the price.

The very first book to be on the book chain was:

A Little Life by Hanya Yanagihara

The second book to be on the book chain was:

The Order of Time by Carlo Rovelli

The third book to be on the book chain was:

the four by Scott Galloway

The fourth book to be on the book chain was:

How to win friends and influence people by Dale Carnegie
