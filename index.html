The Bookchain Project

"Books are the quietest and most constant of friends; they are the most accessible and wisest of counselors, and the most patient of teachers."
- Charles William Eliot

I set up this site for the bookchain project. I am planning to later merge this with my personal website.

These days, everyone is talking about blockchain. Unfortunately, I don't know much about it. But I am interested in books. So Nikolaj Ramsdal Nielsen, a good friend of mine and I decided that we can do something similar.

The Bookchain

The bookchain is quite a simple concept. One person reads a book, signs it on the title page, and gives it to the next person. Once the book reaches 20 signatures, it is planned to return to the first reader. Just imagine what it would be like to realize that the book you loved traveled to distant places held in the hands of so many people and was loved. Isn't that a bit more exciting than a bitcoin that became absolutely no value after you followed the whole blockchain hype?
