A word from Jaehurn: This was the very first book that motivated me to start the Bookchain project. Nikolaj Nielsen, a very good friend of mine, gave me this book at the end of his trip in Seoul(Summer, 2019) since he could not carry this book back home. It was the longest English book I have read and possibly one of the longest novels I have read in my life. This book reignited my love for novels, which had been out of my hands for quite a while. With thanks to Nikolaj.
