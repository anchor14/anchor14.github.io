The Runaway Species: How Human Creativity Remakes the World by Anthony Brandt and David Eagleman

I went to the bookstore in the department store near the train station near my base. I was originally planning to buy another book which is the The Design of Everyday Things by Don Norman Unfortunately they did not have this book so I felt like searching another book that was intersting. I encountered this book with an interesting cover and as I flew through the pages I saw the painting Guernica by Picasso, one of my favorite paintings. I instantly decided to buy the book. I met corporal Chun at 2nd Operational Command. He seemed to be interested in the book when we bumped in on our way to work. I gave him the book on 1/31/2019. Fun fact is that both of us are from 28th regiment 3rd battalio almost exactly a year apart.
