Circles


Beautiful things are not the only ones with beauty, and not all the red roses have romance. The Christmas tree has way too many memories, and the New year leaves way too many promises


Anyways, the full moon will go back to darkness, the stars will shower, and wishes will be made.

Purple sky, orange sea, green clouds, pink trees. We imagine them, we paint them, we dream them..


All the dreams that never stopped, All the boys who became men, and the men who stayed as boys. All the girls who became mothers, and all the mothers who finally became daughters.


The flowers always bloom on the same spot, and the seasons always change around this time.


Not all the fires burn my heart, and not all the wines bring love. The scent of my perfume is already gone around the sunset anyways.


The trees will stay green, but I will grow old. The trees will turn red, and I shall stay young.


The potentials that were buried, and the youth that was wasted. The sacrifices made for the beloved, and the young madness that was enjoyed.


The roads that were taken, the roads that were left untouched.


So, let me just love the morning sunrise, the afternoon coffee, the evening melodies. Blue sky, blue sea, white clouds, and green trees. I imagine them, I see them, I love them.
